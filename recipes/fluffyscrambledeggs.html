Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

Easy yet Tasty, meet fluffy scrambled eggs

Classic, Tasty and full of protein, scrambled eggs are a great way to start your day or have a quick meal

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

How To:

  1. In a small bowl mix 3 eggs with half a cup of greek yogurt (the yogurt is what will make the eggs so fluffy)
  2. Put some oil in a pan and turn on the heat
  3. Once the oil is hot put your mixture of eggs and yogurt in the pan and put the heat on low
  4. Gently stir with a wooden spoon to avoid damaging the pan
  5. The eggs will slowly cook, turn off the heat when they reach your preferred texture, we recommend making them soft
  6. Serve on a plate and garnish with chives

Congratulations, you have just made a delicious dish of scrambled eggs that is also rich in proteins!
