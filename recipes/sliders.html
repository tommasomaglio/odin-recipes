Sliders

Sliders, Burgers but better

Smaller than burgers but just as tasty if not more, sliders make for the perfect appetizer whether you are sharing them or eating them alone.

Ingredients

  • 80-20 Ground beef
  • Eggs
  • Salt&Pepper
  • Spices of your choice
  • Hawaiian Rolls
  • Onions
  • Butter
  • American Cheese

How To:

  1. In a bowl put ground beef, 1 egg, diced onions, salt, pepper and spices of your choice
  2. Mix thouroughly and form some 1.5 oz patties
  3. Put some butter in a pan and turn on the heat to melt it
  4. Once the butter is melted put the patties in the pan and let them brown on one side, then flip and repeat adding a slice of american cheese
  5. Once your sliders are cooked take the out of the pan and toast you Hawaiian Rolls in the same pan
  6. Once the buns are toasted take them out and put the burger in between them
  7. Serve on a plate and enjoy
