Pasta with Tomato sauce

Introduction to the Pasta with tomato sauce, also called Neapolitan Pasta

The Pasta with tomato sauce is one the most common, yet delicious dish in the italian cuisine, made from cheap, easy to find ingredients, it does not take much time or skills to make, but if cooked right can satisfy every palate.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Basil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Oil
  • Garlic

How To:

  1. Get a pot and fill it with water
  2. Get a pan and put some oil and a clove of garlic in it

    3. The gas should be set at medium-low heat for the pan and high heat for the pot

  3. Once the water is almost boiling salt it with coarse salt and wait
  4. Once the garlic has browned put your tomato sauce in the pan alongside some basil, be careful as the oil is very hot
  5. Once the water is boiling put your pasta in, cook it for 1-2 minutes less than what it says on the box
  6. Once your pasta is cooked toss it in the pan with the sauce and stir until well mixed
  7. Serve on a plate and garnish with two basil leaves

You now have a delicious Pasta with tomato sauce, made the true italian way!
